Who is the app for?

For news consumers

If you use social media to find the latest news.

Choose your favorite news channels, websites, or people's profiles and receive all their posts in one place.

It doesn't matter whether it's a publication from Inst, FB, or a web page - the app will combine them into one feed.

For Specialists

If you're a specialist who values staying updated on publications from others in your field.

Monitor your competitors Stay updated on trends in your niche Save time

By receiving a daily summary of relevant publications, you won't have the opportunity to be distracted by unrelated posts and advertisements.

For Users with Busy Schedules

If you want to manage your time efficiently, without wasting it on doom scrolling.

Choose your favorite profiles of your friends or popular bloggers. Sort updates by your interests. For example, News, Travel, Fashion, Cuisine, etc.

During the day, when you're on the go or just have some free moments - quickly scroll through the summary and stay informed of all news.
